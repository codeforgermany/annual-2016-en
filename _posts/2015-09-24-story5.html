Air pollution in Stuttgart

‹ ›

Despite efforts of environmental organizations, the European limit for atmospheric particulates is regularly exceeded in Stuttgart. To tackle the problem, the OK Lab developed sensors that collect particulate matter data at home. Self-made devices measure air quality and automatically upload it over the internet. The project has raised enought funds to build 300 devices around the city.
